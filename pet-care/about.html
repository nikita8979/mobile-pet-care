A few words about us

We are locally owned and operated. We offer in-home pet sitting services, pet grooming, and dog walking services.

Diva Dog Pet Care is focused on superior care for your pet. Our main focus is keeping your pet comfortable, and what better way to do that than to keep them in familiar surroundings. We offer in-home services, as well as boarding in our comfortable kennels for those times when you can't take your fur-baby with you.
